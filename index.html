Features

What RTPP can do for you

Simplicity

You just enter the session name to view the votes. To vote you use the extreme simple android app.

Remote team

Planning stories when your team is not physically in the sample room is never easy. RTPP solve this problem thanks to the Android mobile app.

Computer break

Team members will not need to bring their laptops during planning session. All they need is their android phone.

No influence

RTPP will not show the choosed cards until everyone in the session has voted.
